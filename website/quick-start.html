Quick Start

Welcome. Here’s what just happened.

You just bought HiViews. Behind the scenes, an AI agent is being spun up just for you. Here’s how to find it, what to do first, and when you’ll see your first video.

1Your four channels

HiViews talks to you in four places. You don’t need to use all of them, but here’s where each lives:

📧

Email

Magic-link sign-in to the portal, onboarding emails, renewal notices.

🖥️

Portal

hiviews.ai/portal — visual control: avatars, brand, scout inbox, videos.

💬

Telegram

Chat directly with your AI agent. Approve scripts, ask questions, get updates.

📝

Brand form

5-minute questionnaire — teaches your agent who you are.

💡 First thing to look for

Within 5 minutes of purchase, check your email for a magic link from HiViews. Click it to land in your portal. Around the same time, your Telegram bot will DM you. Both are normal — either one works to get started.

2Three things to do today

  1. Fill out your brand questionnaire

    5 minutes, one page. Tells your agent your niche, audience, voice, and what success looks like. Without this, your reels will be generic.

    Open the questionnaire →

  2. Choose your avatar

    Your AI reels need a face. Options:

    • Photo avatar (fastest) — upload a clear selfie
    • Stock avatar (easiest) — pick from HeyGen’s UGC catalog
    • Digital Twin (best quality, Dominate plan) — 30-60s training video

    Go to Avatars →

  3. Review your first scout picks (tomorrow morning)

    Scout runs overnight (~1:30am your local time) and surfaces viral reels worth adapting for your niche. You’ll wake up to a fresh inbox of scored picks, each with a rewritten script ready to produce.

    Go to Scout →

3Your first video

Once you pick an avatar and promote a scout pick (either in the portal or by telling your Telegram agent), the full pipeline kicks in:

What happens behind the scenes

→ Script rewrite (~30s) — your agent tunes the viral script to your brand voice.
→ Avatar render (3-8 min) — HeyGen generates a reel with your avatar speaking the script.
→ Branded wrapper (soon) — logo, handle, end-card.
→ Publish (immediate or on schedule) — pushed live to your connected Instagram.

Total: under 10 minutes from promote to published reel.

4If something looks wrong

⚠️ Magic link never arrived

Supabase (our auth provider) caps email sends. If you refresh or hit “resend” quickly, you’ll get rate-limited and wait ~60s. Give it 2 minutes, then check spam. Still nothing? Email support@hiviews.ai.

⚠️ Telegram bot didn’t say hi

Your bot username is on your portal dashboard once you’re logged in. Open it manually in Telegram, send /start, and you’re connected.

⚠️ Dashboard says “Provisioning…” forever

Most provisioning finishes in 3–5 minutes. If it’s stuck past 10 minutes, something went sideways. Email support@hiviews.ai with your email on file — we’ll re-run it in a minute.

💡 Talk to us

We respond to support@hiviews.ai within a few hours on business days. You can also DM your agent on Telegram and say “tell Brian about X” — it’ll relay.
